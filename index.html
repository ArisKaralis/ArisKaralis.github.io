Welcome!

Hello! I'm Aris Karalis, an Artificial Intelligence Master's student at the University of Southampton, building upon a First Class Honours BSc in Computer Science from the University of Portsmouth. I'm passionate about leveraging advanced AI technologies to create innovative and impactful solutions for real-world problems.

My current focus and expertise include Large Language Models (LLMs), particularly in fine-tuning them and developing sophisticated chatbots with Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG). A recent project in this area involves creating a RAG system for querying and interacting with a personal library of research papers. You can explore this project further on GitHub: ResearchPaperRAG.

During my MSc in AI at the University of Southampton, I've delved into modules such as:

  • Intelligent Agents (focusing on game theory and auctions)
  • Foundations of AI
  • Machine Learning Technologies
  • Deep Learning Technologies
  • Data Mining
  • Algorithmic Game Theory
  • Natural Language Processing
These studies have significantly enhanced my understanding and practical skills in cutting-edge AI.

This website serves as a portfolio of my journey, skills, and projects. Please explore my CV and project sections to learn more about my work and aspirations in the field of Artificial Intelligence.
